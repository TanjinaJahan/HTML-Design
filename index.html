Organization history

Immediately after the establishment of the School of India, in 1948, several villages of some villages and surrounding villages took initiative to set up several graduate frames. In this area, the local people of the area paid 1.68 (one acre eighty-eight) ding. The founder of our school, Mr. Chirajul Islam Chowdhury and donor Mr. Chadulah Mia School received the Secondary and Higher Secondary Education Board in 01 01.1166 as Comilla Secondary School.
